The Hooks of the trade.

Depending on the project, you need a specific sized hook. Though it also depends on your guage. Here a few common sizes for crochet hooks.

0.6mm, ideal for delicate lacework and fine thread crochet like doilies and miniature motifs.

2.25mm (B-1). great for fingering weight yarns and detailed amigurumi projects.

3.5mm (E-4), perfect for sport weight yarns and lightweight garments with good drape.

4mm (G-6), versatile for DK yarns, used in scarves, hats, and baby items.

5mm (H-8), ideal for worsted weight yarns, commonly used in blankets and sweaters.

6mm (J-10), great for chunky yarns, producing cozy textures in winter accessories.

8mm (L-11), suited for bulky yarns, perfect for quick projects like cowls and throws.

10mm (N/P-15), excellent for super bulky yarns, creating bold stitches in rugs and chunky blankets.
